ProfileWizard Update Instructions

If you have ProfileWizard v2.9 (or were redirected by ProfileWizard to here) or under follow these steps:

To update the old ProfileWizard on Greasemonkey, uninstall and reinstall from here.

To update the old ProfileWizard on Tampermonkey, first open the userscript editor (Dashboard > ProfileWizard). Then, under the ProfileWizard logo change the update url to the one in the metadata block. Save.

Otherwise follow these instructions:

To update ProfileWizard on Tampermonkey, just check for userscript updates.

To update ProfileWizard on Greasemonkey, uninstall and reinstall from here.
